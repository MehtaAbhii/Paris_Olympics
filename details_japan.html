Notable Achievements

  • Japan won medals in 16 different sports, showcasing their versatility.
  • Wrestling was Japan's most successful sport, with 8 gold medals and 11 total medals.
  • Shinnosuke Oka was Japan's most decorated athlete, winning 3 gold medals and 1 bronze in Gymnastics.
  • Japan won its first-ever Olympic gold medal in Breaking, with Ami Yuasa's victory in the B-Girls event.
  • Japan continued its strong tradition in Judo, winning 8 medals including 3 golds.
